Hi, I'm Paul...

A senior UX designer with over a decade of experience delivering digital products across agency, consultancy and in-house settings. My practice evolved from visual and interactive design into a focus on usability, systems thinking, and product value - often within complex or regulated domains.

I have led UX and UI strategy for large platforms, contributed to the development and maintanance of design systems, and worked closely with engineers and senior stakeholders to ensure design intent is carried through into code execution. My experience spans SaaS, financial services, e-learning, and mobile products, with a consistent emphasis on user-centred outcomes.

Currently my learning focus is around AI-assisted UX workflows and how to utilise the speed of AI to generate user flows and development assets - and the positive impacts that quick iteration and user testing have.
