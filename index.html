Hey there,
glad you dropped in!

I'm Paul, a senior UX designer with over a decade of experience delivering digital products across agency, consultancy, and in-house settings. My practice developed from visual and interactive design into a focus on usability, systems thinking, and product value, often within complex or regulated domains.

I have led UX and UI strategy for large platforms, contributed to the development of design systems, and worked closely with engineers and senior stakeholders to ensure design intent is carried through to execution. My experience spans SaaS, financial services, e-learning, and mobile products, with a consistent emphasis on clarity, scalability, and user-centred outcomes.

I currently work independently and continue to develop my practice, particularly in motion for learning contexts and emerging AI-assisted UX workflows. I am seeking a senior role within a product-led organisation that values thoughtful design, collaboration, and meaningful impact.
